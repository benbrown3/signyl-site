Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Who We Are

Signyl ("we," "us," "our") is a marketing intelligence company. We operate signyl.agency, signylads.com, signylseo.com, and related services. Our platform uses machine learning to analyze advertising and marketing data on behalf of our clients.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information: name, email address, company name, and role when you create an account or contact us.
  • Advertising platform data: campaign performance data, ad creative metadata, and analytics from connected platforms (Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Amazon Ads) accessed through authorized API integrations on behalf of our clients.
  • Usage data: how you interact with our platform, including pages visited, features used, and device information.
  • Communications: messages you send to us through our contact forms or email.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our marketing intelligence services.
  • To generate ML-powered insights, anomaly detection, forecasts, and optimization recommendations from connected advertising data.
  • To communicate with you about your account, our services, and updates.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

Data From Advertising Platforms

When clients connect their advertising accounts (Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Amazon Ads), we access campaign performance data through official platform APIs using OAuth authentication. We do not store raw ad creative content. We access only the data necessary to provide our analytics and optimization services, and we process this data in accordance with each platform's API terms of service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With service providers who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics).
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), secure API authentication (OAuth 2.0), access controls, and regular security reviews. Client advertising data is isolated per tenant.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When a client relationship ends, we delete associated advertising data within 90 days unless legal obligations require longer retention.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us. If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to object to processing. California residents have rights under CCPA.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and platform functionality. We use Google Analytics (GA4) for usage analytics. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "last updated" date.
